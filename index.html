About me

I am a current M.Sc. student at McGill University studying Computer Science. My interests range from computer vision to human cognition to pedagogy. I enjoy learning how to learn and applying my knowledge from cognitive science to better understand human and machine behaviour.

Outside of academics, I am a violinist, composer, and artist. Wherever I can, I enjoy combining my skills and computer science with arts and music.

I am currently learning full-stack web development, which is, in my opinion, a great way to use my coding and artistic skills together to form useful and aesthetic websites.
